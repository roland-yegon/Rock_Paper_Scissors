ROCK PAPER SCISSORS DELUXE

Welcome to Rock paper scissors deluxe, a game build by Roland to bring fun

HOW TO PLAY

In this game of Rock, Paper, Scissors, you’ll face off against the computer. Choose your move—rock, paper, or scissors—then the computer will randomly select its own.

RULES WHEN PLAYING

  • Rock beats scissors
  • Scissors beats paper
  • Paper beats rock

If player and computer picks the same thing, then it a tie
