HOW TO PLAY

In this game of Rock, Paper, Scissors, you’ll face off against the computer. Choose your move—rock, paper, or scissors—then the computer will randomly select its own. Rock beats scissors, scissors beats paper, and paper beats rock. If both picks match, the round ends in a tie. Try to outsmart the computer and rack up wins!
